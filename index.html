About Innuelo Consultancy

Our Story

Founded in 2018, INNUELO CONSULTANCY SERVICES began with a simple mission: to empower Ghanaian businesses with the expertise and tools they need to thrive in an increasingly complex business environment. What started as a small team of financial experts has grown into a comprehensive consultancy serving clients across West Africa.

Our name "Innuelo" comes from the Akan word meaning "knowledge" - reflecting our commitment to providing insightful, knowledge-based solutions that drive real business results.

Our Mission

To transform the business landscape in Ghana by providing accessible, world-class consultancy services that enable SMEs to overcome challenges, capitalize on opportunities, and achieve sustainable growth.

Our Values

Innovation

Continuously developing new approaches to solve business challenges

Integrity

Uncompromising ethical standards in all our engagements

Excellence

Commitment to delivering exceptional results for every client

Collaboration

Working as partners with our clients to achieve shared success

Our Team

INNUELO brings together a diverse team of certified accountants, business strategists, tax experts, and financial analysts with extensive experience across multiple industries. Our consultants average 10+ years of experience and hold advanced certifications including:

  • Chartered Accountants (CA, Ghana)
  • Certified Public Accountants (CPA)
  • Chartered Financial Analysts (CFA)
  • Certified Management Accountants (CMA)
  • Blockchain and Cryptocurrency Experts
